Smaller transistors are closer together. Electricity travels shorter distances.

What is a Transistor?

Think of it as a tiny light switch.
On (1) or Off (0).
Millions of these switches flipping together creates logic, math, and video games.

What is the "Law"?

Gordon Moore predicted we would find ways to make these switches half the size every two years, doubling power for the same price.
