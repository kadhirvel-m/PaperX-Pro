The Coach & The Athlete

Imagine you are training an athlete for the Olympics.

The Coach (You)

You decide the Training Regimen. You set the rules before practice starts: "Run 10 laps," "Rest for 5 minutes," "Focus on speed."

THIS IS A HYPERPARAMETER

The Athlete (The Model)

The athlete's body adapts during practice. Their Muscle Mass and Heart Rate change automatically in response to your regimen.

THIS IS A PARAMETER

Under the Hood

The Golden Rule

In Machine Learning, the distinction is about control.

Hyperparameters External Configuration. Set by the human engineer before training.
Parameters Internal State. Learned by the model from the data during training.

The Formula

  • θ = Parameter (Weights)
  • α = Hyperparameter (Learning Rate)
